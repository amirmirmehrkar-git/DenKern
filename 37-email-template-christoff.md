## Strategic Reminder

At this stage, the biggest mistake is trying to validate whether the solution is good.

The goal is to validate whether the problem is important enough that someone would pay to solve it.

## How To Ask The Insurance Question (In The Session)

Avoid:

- Do you think insurance is also interesting?

Prefer:

- We are debating whether to stay close to operators or move toward risk-bearing entities like insurers. From your experience, which path has a clearer and faster validation path?

## Why This Email Works (Short)

- Problem-first, not AI-first
- Early but focused
- Questions are sharp and actionable
- Asks for experience, not surface-level opinions
- Professional tone without sounding needy
