# DenkKern

Decision support for logistics disruptions

## Overview

DenkKern is a decision-support system designed to help logistics and operations teams respond to supply chain disruptions more quickly and more confidently.

When events such as port closures, severe delays, strikes, weather disruptions, or geopolitical risks affect shipments, decision-makers are often forced to act under time pressure, uncertainty, and incomplete information. In those moments, the challenge is not simply detecting the disruption; it is choosing the best response.

DenkKern addresses that problem by structuring the core response options logistics managers already consider, evaluating them across key operational and business dimensions, and presenting them in a ranked, explainable format.

## The Problem

Disruption response in logistics is often fragmented, reactive, and difficult to document. Teams must weigh trade-offs between delivery time, cost impact, operational feasibility, contractual obligations, and downstream business consequences, often without a clear framework for comparing alternatives.

This becomes especially critical when a delayed or disrupted shipment can lead to production interruptions, inventory shortages, customer penalties, or legal exposure.

## The Solution

DenkKern supports managers during disruption events by turning uncertainty into a structured decision process.

The platform is designed to:

- Identify and register disruption events
- Structure the main response scenarios available to the team
- Evaluate each scenario across time, cost, legal exposure, and operational risk
- Present options in a clear, comparable, and explainable way

The system does not automate the final decision. Instead, it helps managers make faster, better-informed, and more defensible choices under pressure.

## Core Use Case

Our initial focus is on logistics disruption response in shipment-dependent environments, especially where delays create significant downstream business impact.

Example situations include:

- Port closures or severe congestion
- Rerouting decisions in maritime logistics
- Inbound shipment delays affecting production or inventory availability
- Alternative port and inland transport decisions
- Urgent partial expediting for critical goods

## Why It Matters

The value of DenkKern lies in helping organizations move from ad hoc disruption management to structured decision support.

This creates benefits such as:

- Faster response times
- Better comparison of trade-offs
- Reduced cost of poor or delayed decisions
- Clearer communication across operational and commercial stakeholders
- More defensible decisions in high-risk situations

## Product Direction

DenkKern combines disruption monitoring, structured scenario analysis, and probabilistic evaluation to support response planning in high-pressure logistics environments.

Our current focus is on validating the decision logic, scenario structure, and workflow needed to make disruption response more systematic and usable in real operational settings.
