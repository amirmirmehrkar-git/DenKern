# Open Strategic Questions (Pressure Tests)

Purpose: these are not operational tasks. They are the *strategic* questions Nick should validate with real operators/buyers to de-risk MVP scope, ICP, trust, and pricing.

## 1) North Star Metric Validation

OQ-001: Can customers realistically quantify cost-of-delay in a defensible way?

OQ-002: How do customers currently estimate production downtime costs?

OQ-003: Who inside the customer organization owns the cost-of-delay number?

OQ-004: Can a customer calibrate a basic cost model within 1-2 weeks without consulting?

## 2) MVP Scope Validation

OQ-005: Is port congestion alone painful enough to justify a pilot, or must MVP also include strikes/weather/customs/vessel rerouting?

OQ-006: Can customers accept manual data intake during the pilot phase, or do they require ERP integration to buy?

OQ-007: What is the minimum operational workflow customers need before they consider the product useful (dashboard vs recommendation vs actions like expedite/sourcing)?

## 3) Actionability Validation

OQ-008: When a disruption happens today, what actions do operations managers actually take?

OQ-009: Which decisions are currently manual, stressful, or financially risky?

OQ-010: What decisions are still made through Excel, email, or phone calls?

OQ-011: Which mitigation options are realistically available during disruptions (wait, expedite, replacement supplier, reroute, production adjustment)?

## 4) Trust and Explainability

OQ-012: What level of explainability is required before customers trust recommendations?

OQ-013: Would customers trust probabilistic recommendations, or do they demand deterministic outputs?

OQ-014: Do customers require audit trails for operational decisions?

## 5) GTM / Buyer Validation

OQ-015: Who is the actual economic buyer (COO, Head of Ops, Supply Chain Director, Plant Manager, CFO)?

OQ-016: Who feels the pain most intensely (pain owner != budget owner)?

OQ-017: What is the expected pilot buying process?

## 6) Security and Adoption

OQ-018: What data are customers unwilling to share during a pilot?

OQ-019: Would customers allow cloud-hosted shipment intelligence, or do they require on-prem?

## 7) Commercial Validation

OQ-020: What financial outcome would make the pilot obviously worth paying for?

OQ-021: Would customers pay for prediction, recommendation, avoided downtime, or operational visibility?

## 8) Beachhead Validation

OQ-022: Which industry has the best combo of highest downtime cost, fastest buying cycle, and simplest onboarding?

Candidate beachheads:

- shipbuilding
- chemicals
- machinery
- pharma
- automotive suppliers

## Single Most Important Question

If we only validate one question:

OQ-001 (reframed): Can we get a customer to a defensible cost-of-delay model within two weeks, without heavy integration or consulting?

If YES: startup is viable.
If NO: value proposition weakens significantly.
