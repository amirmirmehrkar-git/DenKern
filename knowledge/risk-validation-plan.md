# Risk Validation Plan (Framework)

This is the working template Nick should fill for each open question (OQ-###). The goal is to make discovery *testable* and evidence-based.

## Suggested Validation Methods

- 1:1 operator interviews (primary)
- buyer interviews (secondary, but important)
- review of a real past disruption case (post-mortem style)
- pilot pre-mortem: "what would stop you from trying this?"
- pricing pressure-test: "what would you pay for a pilot if it saves X?"

## Minimum Evidence Standard (For High-Risk Questions)

For any question marked High/Existential:

- at least 5 independent interviews
- at least 2 different companies
- capture quotes + concrete numbers where possible
