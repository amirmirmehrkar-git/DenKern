# 10-Day MVP Plan - Nick (Business, Customer Discovery and GTM)

Primary responsibility: customer validation, onboarding strategy, and commercial preparation.

## A) Customer Discovery

### Day 1-2 - Define ICP Hypothesis

Target customers:

- Hamburg manufacturers
- high downtime industries
- inbound shipment dependency

Examples:

- shipbuilding
- chemicals
- machinery
- industrial manufacturing

### Day 2-4 - Create Interview Questionnaire

Questions about:

- shipment delays
- operational pain
- financial impact
- current tools
- escalation process

### Day 2-5 - Conduct Customer Interviews

Goal: 10-15 interviews.

Focus:

- operational disruptions
- downtime costs
- current workflows
- manual decision-making
- willingness to pay

## B) Market and Pricing Validation

### Day 5-7 - Validate Pricing

Test:

- pilot pricing
- monthly SaaS assumptions
- willingness to pay

Suggested pilot range: EUR 5k-15k.

## C) Pre-Sales and Customer Pipeline

### Day 6-10 - Pre-Sales Pipeline

Create:

- customer outreach list
- pilot customer shortlist
- pre-sales conversations
- follow-up process

### Day 8-10 - Draft Pilot Agreement

Simple pre-contract including:

- pilot duration
- limited scope
- feedback collaboration
- no production SLA
