# Business Plan

DenkKern  
AI-Assisted Operational Decision Intelligence for Supply Chain Disruptions

## 1. Executive Summary

### Company Overview

DenkKern is an AI-assisted decision-support platform designed to help manufacturing and logistics companies respond to shipment disruptions and operational uncertainty.

Instead of only tracking shipments or predicting delays, DenkKern evaluates operational response scenarios, estimates financial impact, and recommends the best business action.

The platform combines:

- Real-time logistics signals
- Probabilistic risk modeling
- Operational cost analysis
- Human-centered decision support

to reduce downtime losses and improve operational resilience.

### Core Problem

Manufacturers and logistics teams currently rely on:

- Fragmented data
- Reactive workflows
- Manual judgment

during disruptions such as:

- Vessel delays
- Port congestion
- Strikes
- Weather incidents

Existing systems provide visibility, but not decision support.

### Core Solution

DenkKern transforms shipment uncertainty into financially informed operational decisions.

The system:

- Detects disruption risks
- Predicts delay probabilities
- Compares operational response options
- Calculates business impact
- Recommends the optimal action

### Initial Target Market

Specialized mid-sized industrial and logistics companies (Mittelstand) in Germany, especially around Hamburg.

Initial focus:

- Manufacturing firms
- Industrial importers
- Specialized freight forwarders

with high downtime costs and operational sensitivity.

## 2. Market Opportunity

### Industry Context

Global supply chains have become increasingly unstable due to:

- Geopolitical disruptions
- Climate-related events
- Labor strikes
- Regulatory changes
- Congestion volatility

At the same time:

- Manufacturers face higher operational dependency on just-in-time logistics
- Downtime costs continue to rise

### Market Gap

Current solutions focus primarily on:

- Tracking
- Visibility
- Alerts
- Analytics

But few systems answer:

> What should we do now?

This creates a gap between prediction and operational decision-making. DenkKern positions itself in this gap.

## 5. Example Use Case

### Lena 2.0

A Hamburg shipbuilding company depends on marine-quality bolts arriving on time. A maritime disruption threatens the shipment.

Every day of delay costs:

```text
EUR 150,000 in lost production
```

DenkKern:

- Predicts delay probability
- Compares mitigation options
- Calculates expected losses
- Recommends replacement sourcing

Result:

- Reduced downtime
- Lower financial loss
- Faster decisions
