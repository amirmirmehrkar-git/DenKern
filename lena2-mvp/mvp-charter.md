# Lena 2.0 MVP

The MVP niche is a manufacturing disruption use case in Hamburg.

Lena is a production manager at a shipbuilding firm. A critical shipment of marine-quality bolts may arrive late because of maritime or port disruption. Every delayed day costs the company EUR 150k in lost production.

## Core User

Lena needs to decide what action to take before the disruption fully materializes.

She does not need another passive tracking dashboard. She needs a structured comparison of operational options, financial impact, uncertainty, and recommended ranking.

## Core Situation

- Critical shipment: marine-quality bolts.
- Location context: Hamburg / shipbuilding manufacturing.
- Disruption type: maritime or port delay.
- Business impact: EUR 150k lost production per delayed day.

## Golden Narrative Details

Source: Amir relaying / discussing the project scenario with the AI.

Lena works for a shipbuilding firm in Hamburg. A critical shipment of marine-quality bolts is at risk. The shipping company says the parts will arrive on the 15th, but Lena worries that disruptions will delay the shipment. Each delayed day creates EUR 150k in lost production.

The ship is currently off the southern coast of Spain and has expected stops in Porto, London, Amsterdam, and Hamburg. There is also possible traffic in Amsterdam and a strike in Hamburg.

## Versioned Location Notes

- Version 1: The disruption is described as being off the coast of France.
- Version 2: The expected disruption is described as being off the coast of Spain.

Both versions should remain in the knowledge base until the team resolves which geography is canonical for the demo.

## Outcome In Reference Story

Lena orders a week's worth of replacement parts from a warehouse in Poland. They arrive on the 16th, one day behind schedule, while the ship arrives on the 20th. The firm loses only one day of production and saves roughly EUR 450k, minus emergency shipment and replacement costs.

## MVP Discipline

If a feature does not help validate this first pilot workflow, it is not MVP.

The Lena use case is the beachhead. Expansion comes only after this decision workflow is validated.
